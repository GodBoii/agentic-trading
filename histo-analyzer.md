### **AGENT 1: Historical Data Analysis**

**What to Analyze:**

**Price Pattern Recognition:**
- Support/Resistance levels (last 1M, 3M, 6M)
- Historical price reactions at these levels
- Seasonal patterns (day of week, time of day edge)
- Gap behavior (gap fill statistics)

**Volume Profile Analysis:**
- Volume-weighted average price (VWAP) pivots
- High Volume Nodes (HVN) and Low Volume Nodes (LVN)
- Volume distribution at price levels

**Statistical Properties:**
- Mean reversion tendency (Hurst exponent)
- Volatility regime (high/low/transitional)
- Correlation with index/sector over different periods
- Typical intraday patterns (opening range, lunch lull, closing surge)

**Historical Event Analysis:**
- Past earnings reactions
- Dividend ex-date behaviors
- Split/bonus history impact

**Output:** Summary with confidence scores for each finding
