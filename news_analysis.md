### **AGENT 5: News Analysis**

**Data Sources:**
- NSE announcements
- BSE filings
- MoneyControl, Economic Times, Bloomberg Quint
- Twitter/X sentiment (Fintwit)
- Company press releases

**Analysis Methods:**

**Sentiment Classification:**
- Positive/Negative/Neutral scoring
- Entity recognition (is this news about our stock?)
- Severity scoring (routine vs material news)

**Event Detection:**
- Earnings announcements
- Management changes
- Legal issues
- Contract wins/losses
- Regulatory actions

**Competitive Intelligence:**
- News about competitors
- Sector-wide news impact

**Temporal Analysis:**
- News freshness (15min, 1hr, 1day old)
- Market reaction to similar past news

**Critical:** Set up real-time news feeds with sub-minute latency. By the time retail news hits, institutions have already traded.
